# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

German business owners, marketing leads, founders, and local service teams who need one agency to connect search visibility, paid acquisition, social presence, and digital design.

## Product Purpose

The site presents Digitalwerk as a serious, creative, full-funnel digital marketing agency. The homepage variants act as promotional entry points, while concrete service, pricing, case, and contact information lives on linked subpages.

## Brand Personality

Precise, inventive, commercially grounded.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS gradients, endless identical service cards, stock agency buzzwords, anonymous blue corporate layouts, and one-page-only brochure behavior.

## Design Principles

- Show the system behind growth, not isolated tactics.
- Make every route feel like a distinct campaign concept while keeping one shared agency voice.
- Use German copy that sounds confident, local, and practical.
- Keep service depth discoverable through navigation instead of burying everything in one long scroll.
- Let motion and visuals clarify relationships between channels.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, readable German line lengths, keyboard-accessible navigation, resilient responsive layouts, and reduced-motion alternatives for decorative movement.
